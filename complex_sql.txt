15)	 FIND THE MAX 3 SALARIES FROM EMPLOYEE TABLE.
mysql> SELECT DISTINCT SALARY
    -> FROM EMPLOYEES A
    -> WHERE 3>= (SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT SALARY)
    -> FROM EMPLOYEES B
    -> WHERE B.SALARY >= A.SALARY)
    -> ORDER BY A.SALARY DESC;
